Kakigōri (Shaved Ice)

Shaved Ice or Kakigōri is a cold, fluffy treat that is best eaten during hot days. The best part is that you only need 3 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Syrup
  • Toppings
  • Condensed Milk - Optional, use for a sweeter taste.

Instructions

Set your ice shaving machine aside and get your ice mold ready.

  1. Fill your ice mold with water and put it in the freezer - make sure the ice will fit in the machine. To make life easier, you could finish this step the day before making Kakigōri.
  2. Once the ice is ready put it in the shaving machine, place a bowl underneath - to catch the fluffy shavings - and start shaving the ice.
  3. The last step is to drizzle your favorite syrup and add the toppings. Feel free to decorate it however you like.

Tools Used

The ice shaving machine can be bought online or you could use a grater instead.

Toppings

Here are common toppings you could use:

  • Strawberry
  • Blueberry
  • Mango
  • Pineapple
  • Kiwi
