A Recipe For Dr. Pepper Brownies

I recently learned of a new way to make unbelievably delicious brownies and the secret.... Dr. Pepper. Yes, the soda! Come along with me on this journey of chocolatey bliss.

This recipe comes from a viral video, so if you've already seen it a thousand times, my apologies. Let's dive right in.

Ingredients

For this recipe, we will be keeping things as simple as possible, so while it may be even better to create the brownie mix from scratch, we will be using a store bought brownie mix (any mix should do the job).

  • Pre-made brownie mix - 1 bag
  • Eggs - 2
  • Vegetable Oil - 1/2 cup
  • Chocoalte Chips - Add as desired if not included in the mix
  • Dr. Pepper - 1/4 cup
  • Softened Butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees
  2. Add dry ingredients to a large mixing bowl (brownie mix and chocolate chips)
  3. Add eggs, vegetable oil, and Dr. Pepper to the dry mix
  4. Mix and fold ingredients together using a spatula (or optionally an electric mixer if you have it)
  5. Get a 9x9 inch glass baking dish and coat the inside with the softened butter
  6. Pour your brownie mix into the dish
  7. Once the oven reaches 350 degrees, place the dish in the oven and bake for 20 minutes
  8. Take the brownies out of the oven after 20 minutes and test the consistency with a toothpick. \ The toothpick should not pick up any crumbs when stuck into the center of the brownies and removed.
  9. Let the brownies cool for a few minutes and enjoy!

And there you have it. A delicious recipe for easy to make brownies when you need a little sweet treatment!

Alterations

If you are lactose sensitive like I am, you can substitue the butter for a baking spray like Pam, or line the baking dish with parchment paper before putting it in the oven.
