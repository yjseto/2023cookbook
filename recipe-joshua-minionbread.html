Minion Bread (Banana Bread)

Original recipe from here.

It seems that no matter what food buying schedule I try to set, I always have bananas that turn brown REALLY fast. Every single time, I get an ear full from the man friend that we need to do something with the bananas. Therefore, that lead me to making classic banana bread or minion bread in our house. Why minion bread? Well, if you live under a rock then you have never heard of Despicable Me or Minions. In that case, you can go on forever eating banana bread. We will go forth in life eating our minion bread…banana.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp Baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 3/4 cups Flour
  • 3/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 1/2 cups Sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla
  • 1/2 cup EVOO
  • 1/3 cup Water
  • 2 bananas

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.

  1. Mix everything in order of how they are listed in the ingredients.
  2. Fill a baking container halfway full with the mixed ingredients.
  3. Once the oven is ready, place the baking container in the oven.
  4. Let the bread cook for an hour checking the bread every 15 minutes.
  5. Once the tops are golden brown, poke it with a toothpick and if no batter comes out, it is finished
  6. Watch the Minions movie and enjoy the Minion Bread.

Tips

The Minions movie is streaming on Peacock.
